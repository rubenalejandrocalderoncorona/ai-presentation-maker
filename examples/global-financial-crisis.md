  - Housing prices peak and start to fall
  - Foreclosures surge as adjustable rates reset

**Slide 3 — The Instruments: Toxic Assets**
- Type: cards-grid
- Content: 4 cards

  - CDO (orange): Bundled mortgage-backed securities
  - Credit Default Swap (violet): Insurance against default
  - Synthetic CDO (blue): Betting on the outcome of a CDO
- Animation: each card reveals on spacebar press (4 steps)

**Slide 4 — The Climax: Lehman Brothers Collapse**
- Type: timeline
- Key dates (clockwise from top):

  - Sept 12, 2008: Negotiations for Lehman Brothers rescue fail
  - Sept 15, 2008: Lehman Brothers files for bankruptcy
  - Sept 16, 2008: AIG bailout announced
- Animation: each date reveals on spacebar (4 steps)

**Slide 6 — The Aftermath: Policy Response**

- Right panel (Regulation - Dodd-Frank Act):

  - Consumer protection bureau
  - Volcker Rule (limits on proprietary trading)
- Animation: left panel fades in (step 1), right panel fades in (step 2), comparison highlights on step 3
